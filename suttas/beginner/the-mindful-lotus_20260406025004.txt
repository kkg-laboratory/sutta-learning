The Mindful Lotus

The Buddha taught: "Imagine a lotus flower, its roots in the muddy water, yet its petals remain unsoiled. So too, the mindful one remains in the world, yet untouched by its impurities. 

"Just as the lotus blooms in the warmth of the sun, wisdom blooms in the warmth of mindfulness. The roots of suffering are like the muddy water, but with sati, one can rise above, untouched and unspoiled.

"May you cultivate the mindfulness of the lotus, and may your heart bloom with wisdom, free from the impurities of the world."
