# The Mindful Household
In the gentle abode of the awakened heart, mindfulness is the loving guardian that watches over all who reside within. It is the warm breeze that stirs the soul, inviting us to awaken to the present moment.

## Meaning
Mindfulness is the survey of people residing in the home of Buddha, a reminder that our true home is not a physical dwelling, but the sanctuary of our own awareness. As we cultivate mindfulness, we become the guardians of our own hearts, surveying the landscape of our thoughts, emotions, and actions with kindness and compassion.

## Reflection
As we reflect on the notion of mindfulness as the survey of our inner household, we may ask ourselves: What are the inhabitants of my heart? What thoughts, emotions, and desires reside within me? Do I approach them with judgment or with kindness? May our reflection be a gentle rain that nourishes the garden of our soul, allowing us to grow in greater awareness and understanding.

## Practice
To embody the spirit of mindfulness, we may practice the following:
* Take a few moments each day to sit in stillness, surveying the landscape of your heart.
* Gently notice the thoughts, emotions, and sensations that arise, without judgment or attachment.
* As you breathe in, imagine fresh awareness entering your being, and as you breathe out, imagine any tension or stress leaving your body.
* May your practice be a warm embrace that welcomes all aspects of your being, inviting greater peace, clarity, and compassion to reside within.
